This folder contains the ImageNet pertained models. 

They can be downloaded from 
[mobilenet](http://download.tensorflow.org/models/mobilenet_v1_2018_02_22/mobilenet_v1_1.0_224.tgz) and 
[resnet](http://download.tensorflow.org/models/resnet_v2_50_2017_04_14.tar.gz).

They should be put under the directory of "mobilenet_v1" and "resnet_v2_50".
